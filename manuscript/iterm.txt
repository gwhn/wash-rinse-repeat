# iTerm

|`<ESC> d`    | Delete a word                 |
|`<CTR> a`    | Jump to start of line         |
|`<CTR> e`    | Jump to end of line           |
|`<CTR> k`    | Kill line from cursor         |
|`<CTR> y`    | Paste from kill buffer        |
|`<CTR> r`    | Reverse search command history|
|`<CTR> f`    | Move forward one character    |
|`<CTR> b`    | Move back one character       |
|`<ESC> f`    | Move forward one word         |
|`<ESC> b`    | Move back one word            |
